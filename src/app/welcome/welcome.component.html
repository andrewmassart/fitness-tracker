  • The exercises are in on firebase and their are 4 to choose from they each have a duration in seconds. The seconds are converted to a percentage on the screen output and each "step" fills the progress spinner incrementally
  • The data table has sortable headers and a filter input. All users share the data pool so no one has to wait for exercises to finish to use the filter or sort headers
